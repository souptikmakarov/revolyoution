Who we are

This organization came into being on the 2nd of January 2015 with an aim of providing the best we can to the underprivileged kids and to give the best possible resources to then.  We also aim at making the poorer sections of the society aware of the problems regarding hygiene,  proper education and other such factors so that they realise how these can be amended.

Our Vision

India has been developing since last 65 years of independence without any massive development in the edducation policy, infrastructure or oresence of basic amenities in the society.This organisation is a stepping stone to spread knowledge regarding education policies, social hygiene and other problems faced by the society.

Motives

1. Awareness regarding importance of education among budding minds and spreading it among
underprivileged children.

2. To put a check on domestic violence and condition of women

3. Making people aware of the importance of personal hygiene, proper health and related
cleanliness factors.

4. Inculcating values such as self confidence and unity in the masses...and many more...
